Engineering Programs

BS Computer Engineering

As a cyber-security specialist you will be responsible for security operations of customer solutions involving hands-on t roubleshooting, break-fixing, incident management in additional to deployment of customer solutions. The successful candidate will meet the following qualifications.

Responsibilities

Review security-related events, assessing severity, criticality and priority
Monitor and report based on client SLAs with an emphasis on quality and accuracy.
Analyze network traffic to assist in testing new signatures for production deployment to client environments
Deliver cyber security projects for clients demonstrating your experience in multiple cyber domains (e.g., Information security, vulnerability management, IAM, etc.)
Facilitate sessions with clients to identify, scope and refine solutions customized to their business needs.
Facilitate use of technology-based tools or methodologies to review, design and/or implement products and services

Qualification / Experience

Bachelor or Masters degree in Information Security, Computer Science, Engineering.
Certifications such as GIAC, CEH
Demonstrate knowledge of cyber security and technologies such as SIEM, IPS, Cloud security, IAM, Encryption
technologies, Endpoint Security.
